📖 How to Use

1

🔍 Scan Products

Click "Start Scanning" to activate the camera. Position UPC barcodes within the frame to automatically add products to your invoice.

2

📝 Review Items

Each scanned item appears in the invoice list above. You can remove items using the 🗑️ button if needed.

3

💾 Save Invoice

Once all items are scanned, click "Save Invoice" to process the transaction through HelloCash.

4

🔄 Scan More

After saving, click "Scan Another" to add more items or start a new invoice.

💡 Tips

  • Ensure good lighting for better barcode scanning
  • Hold the barcode steady within the camera frame
  • If a product isn't recognized, you can manually select it from the inventory
  • The system automatically calculates tax and totals
